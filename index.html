The Challenge

Industrial networks are
dangerously under-defended

OT/ICS environments face unique threats — heterogeneous device ecosystems, noisy alert signals, and critically short response windows.

⚡

Complex Network Topology

Industrial environments mix PLC, SCADA, ICS, and IoT devices across isolated segments, creating blind spots that traditional tools miss.

📡

IoT Attack Surface

Legacy IoT devices often run outdated firmware with hardcoded credentials, making them easy entry points for lateral movement.

🔔

Alert Fatigue

SOC teams drown in thousands of low-fidelity alerts daily. Without intelligent prioritization, critical threats get buried.

⏱

Slow Manual Response

Manual investigation and response cycles stretch from minutes to hours — far too slow for OT environments where downtime costs thousands per second.

Scope & MVP Definition

What the prototype includes

Detailed scope for the SentinelX MVP — honest about boundaries, demonstrably valuable within them.

✅ MVP Includes

▾
Home page with full product story • Book a Demo flow with lead capture and auto-redirect to platform • Sign In & Registration modals • About Us section • Scope & MVP section • User Stories & Epics section • Interactive threat detection dashboard • Animated threat cards with live simulation • IoT Vulnerability Scanner UI • Network Anomaly Detection visualization • SOC incident feed • AI Cybersecurity Chatbot • Terms & Conditions page

🎯 What This MVP Validates

▾
Product-market fit for industrial cybersecurity • Demo-to-lead conversion flow • UI feasibility for SOC workflows • AI anomaly scoring concept • Executive-friendly risk visibility • Investor-grade platform credibility

🚫 Intentionally Out of Scope

▾
Production SSO / enterprise authentication • Live packet-level OT network ingestion • Multi-tenant billing engine • Real SIEM integrations (Splunk, QRadar, etc.) • Deep forensic tooling • Production-grade incident orchestration • Multi-region deployment

⏱ Why Scope Is Realistic for MVP

▾
Focused on one polished, coherent demo flow rather than spreading across many half-finished features. Simulated data replaces production integrations. UI-first approach lets evaluators immediately see product value. Clear MVP boundaries signal product thinking, not limitation.
